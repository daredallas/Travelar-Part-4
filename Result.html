Collecting Quantitative Results

During the test, the quantitative result that we will be collecting the amount of time it takes to complete each of the tasks. Using a timer on a group member’s phone, we will be watching while they complete the process and recording the time taken for each task. Qualitative Results




What aspects of your preferred version of the UI did you like?

  • That the name of the place was displayed and that you could select tags separately in the first version
  • The pop up menu was non invasive and didn’t take you to a new page in the first version
  • The first version was more intuitive and also had a very familiar layout similar to google and yelp
  • I liked the smaller icons while viewing because it was not very intrusive in the second version



What aspects of your preferred version of the UI did you dislike?

  • The second version simply had exclamation marks, with no real context to them
  • The second version brought you to a new page and I would have preferred a pop up instead
  • the page that showed the top view of the building was confusing in the second version
  • Nothing in particular, I just liked the first version better.



What element of the interface were you confused or when were you confused?

  • There was no similarity option so I was confused that I had to click on the tags in the first version
